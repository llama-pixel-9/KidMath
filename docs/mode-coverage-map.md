# KidMath Mode Coverage Map (K-5)

## Level Band Mapping

- Levels 1-3: `K-1`
- Levels 4-6: `2-3`
- Levels 7-10: `4-5`

## Coverage Matrix

| Mode | Conceptual Focus | Procedural Focus | Application Focus | Key Subskills |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `addition` | part-part-whole, make-ten, missing addend | sum fluency and regrouping readiness | join/combine contexts | `makeTen`, `composeDecompose`, `unknownAddend` |
| `subtraction` | compare/take-away structure, related facts | difference fluency, decomposition | unknown start/change contexts | `differenceAsDistance`, `decomposeToSubtract`, `unknownSubtrahend` |
| `multiplication` | equal groups and arrays | facts fluency and factor reasoning | groups/rate contexts | `equalGroups`, `arrayReasoning`, `factFluency` |
| `division` | inverse relation to multiplication | quotient fluency with whole-number quotients | sharing/grouping contexts | `partitioning`, `inverseFact`, `unknownQuotient` |
| `comparing` | benchmark and number-line comparison | symbol selection (`>`, `<`, `=`) | compare in scenario language | `benchmarkCompare`, `symbolSelection`, `distanceCompare` |
| `counting` | subitizing and structured counting | count sequence and one-to-one correspondence | counting in context (objects/events) | `subitizing`, `countOn`, `cardinality` |
| `skipCounting` | repeated addition pattern structure | next-term fluency | grouped context bridge to multiplication | `patternRule`, `stepInference`, `groupsToProduct` |
| `placeValue` | unitizing tens/hundreds and expanded form | place-value decomposition/composition | contextualized place-value interpretation | `tensOnes`, `expandedForm`, `regroupingSense` |

## Current Strengths and Planned Corrections

- Strength: broad procedural coverage across 8 modes.
- Gap: conceptual/application underrepresented in arithmetic modes.
- Gap: diagnostic granularity missing (`level` only, no subskill state).
- Gap: generic distractors reduce measurement quality.
- Gap: retry context loss for mode-specific display payloads.

## Alignment Expectations by Mode

1. At least 3 subskills tracked per mode.
2. At least 1 blueprint per family in each level band.
3. At least 2 misconception tags used in distractor generation per subskill.
4. At least 1 representation beyond plain symbolic equations per mode.
